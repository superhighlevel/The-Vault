##### Precision
The fraction of correct predictions among examples that are predicted by $f$ to be positive. Preci**SI**on (**SIDE**)$$PRECISION(f):=\dfrac{\sum_{i=1}^N1(f(x_i;\theta)=y_i^*\ AND\ y_i^* = +1)}{\sum_{i=1}^N1(f(x_i;\theta)=+1)}$$
